hurticulture

hurticulture is a coop farming survival horror game. i know, it sounds like word soup!

you and your party are intergalactic farmers whose job is to forage, plant, and harvest to keep yourselves alive.

you must convert some plants into biofuel to keep your base powered, while selling other plants to purchase new seeds, items, tools, vehicles and weapons.

be careful, as once your power runs out, your party will be left defenseless against the monsters that roam the planet.
