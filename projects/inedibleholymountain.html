inedible holy mountain

a mod for the sandbox game noita.

if terrain is broken in the holy mountain, the gods are angered, which summons a giant skeleton guard, stevari, to kill you. some of the enemies, the worms and the lukki, can break terrain, including the holy mountain. this causes the guard to spawn, even though the player isn't the one at fault.

this mod prevents worms and lukki from destroying the holy mountain terrain.


if youre interested in downloading the mod, here it is in the workshop.
